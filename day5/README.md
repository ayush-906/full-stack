## How to Use

1. **Add Items to Cart**: Click "Add to Cart" on any product
2. **View Cart**: Click the cart icon in the navbar
3. **Start Checkout**: Click "Checkout" in the cart modal
4. **Complete Steps**: Follow the 4-step checkout process
5. **Place Order**: Review and confirm your order
